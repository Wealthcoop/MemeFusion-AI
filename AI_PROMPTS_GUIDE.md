## Prompt Engineering Best Practices

### 1. Maintain Pose and Composition
Always include: "Maintain exact pose, body orientation, and facial silhouette"

### 2. Specify Quality Level
Use terms like: "Ultra-realistic 8K render", "Studio quality", "Professional grade"

### 3. Add Lighting Instructions
Examples: "Dramatic rim lighting", "Volumetric lighting", "Cinematic lighting"

### 4. Include Style References
Reference known styles: "Studio Ghibli", "Pixar", "Blade Runner aesthetic"

### 5. Use Technical Terms
For better results: "Subsurface scattering", "Depth-of-field", "Cel-shading"
